Frequently Asked Questions about Subscriptions

How does car subscription work?

It's like a long-term rental but more flexible. You pay a fixed monthly fee to use a car, covering insurance, maintenance, and mileage (up to your plan's limit). You can often swap cars or cancel with notice.

What's included in the monthly fee?

Typically, the car itself, registration, routine maintenance, basic insurance, and a set mileage allowance. Premium plans might include more comprehensive insurance or unlimited mileage.
